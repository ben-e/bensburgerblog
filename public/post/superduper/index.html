superduper

10. May, 2017

Super Duper Burger is…fine. The burgers always look good, sometimes they even taste good. But sometimes they don’t. Better than Locol though.

Somewhat like Locol, Super Duper Burger advocates for change through conscious eating, however, they don’t beat you over the head with it, and they don’t try and act like a hamburger should be considered a health food or a driver of social activism. They just think that food shouldn’t be garbage.

The pickles are good too.

Rank: 1.
